About Me

Hi, this is Emilia! I am a computational biologist-to-be who is passionate about integrating computational technologies to tease apart the world of biology.

More about my research interests and journey here.

Outside of science, I am also a huge fan of cooking, travelling (trying food from different places), exploring the great nature, and meeting new people!


Timeline



I started my undergradate degree at the University of British Columbia!

I decided to major in a combined degree of Microbiology & Immunology and Computer Science.

I joined UBC iGEM, a student-led multidisciplinary research team that aims to leverage synthetic biology to solve problems in the real life. This marked the start of my journey to create biological systems for useful purposes.

I joined Dr. Alex Wyatt's lab as a bioinformatics Co-op student. This marked the start of my research journey.

I joined Dr. Carl de Boer's lab as a directed studies student.

I got into graduate school!
